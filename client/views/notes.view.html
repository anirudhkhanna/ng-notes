ng-notes

A comprehensive note-taking app to quickly capture what is on your mind - ideas, pictures, lists and more. Built with MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, AngularJS and Node.js). The UX is largely based on AngularJS - hence the name ng-notes. ☺
