Crisp, user-friendly design

The welcoming, clutter-free interface keeps you focussed on your work. In a minimal and vibrant space, nothing but productivity flows in.

Perfect for all devices

This app is friends with all your devices. Capture a note once, and access it from any device - be it your laptop, tablet or smartphone.

Multitude of features

Loaded with all the features you crave. Attach images and files, format text, dictate notes and insert emojis on the click of a button.

Navigate your way
