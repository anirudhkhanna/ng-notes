ng-notes

A simple note-taking app to quickly capture what is on your mind - ideas, pictures, lists and more. Built with MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, AngularJS and Node.js). The UX is largely based on AngularJS - hence the name ng-notes. ☺

© 2017 ng-notes  •  Inspired by Google Keep

This is just a front-end demo of the app. The back-end systems are taking a nap. This means that you won't be able to sign-up or permanently save any notes here. Apologies!
